Our Simple 6-Step Process

From browsing to submission-ready delivery — we've made it seamless for students.

1
📋

Browse & Select

Explore our catalog of 200+ projects. Filter by branch (CSE, ECE, EEE, Mech, DS, AI/ML), difficulty level, project type, and your budget. Found something? Shortlist it — or tell us your topic and we'll suggest the best match.

2
💬

Discuss & Customise

WhatsApp or call us to discuss your requirements — tech stack preferences, college guidelines, submission deadline, team size, and any specific features you need. We'll confirm feasibility and suggest the right package. Zero-pressure, no obligation.

3
💳

Confirm & Pay

Once agreed, you pay 50% advance via UPI, bank transfer, PhonePe, or GPay. You'll receive a payment receipt and a project agreement document with scope, timeline, and deliverables clearly stated.

4
🔨

Development

Our team builds your project from scratch — industry-grade code, modular architecture, proper error handling, and best practices. You'll receive progress updates via WhatsApp. We handle testing, debugging, and integration.

5
📦

Delivery

On delivery day you receive everything: complete source code (GitHub or ZIP), project report, PPT, demo video, deployment guide, and research paper draft (Premium). Pay the remaining 50% after reviewing the deliverables.

6
🎓

Presentation Support

We don't leave you after delivery. We help you prepare for viva questions, explain every part of the code so you can answer confidently, review your presentation, and provide 15–45 days of post-delivery bug-fixing support.
